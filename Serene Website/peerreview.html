Peer Review

Peer review is a vital process ensuring the quality of accepted and published papers. Experts review submissions to assess relevance, originality, and presentation. Authors receive feedback to refine their papers before publication.

The Journal uses a double-blind peer review process to ensure fair editorial decisions. Reviewers can accept or reject submissions based on merit. The Editorial Team, following reviewer recommendations, may accept, request revisions, or reject submissions.
